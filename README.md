# Chart 2: Borrow & Supply Trends (Utilization Rate Framework)
<img width="3058" height="1845" alt="cum-supply-borrow-USD-permarket-ethereum" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fe180e5f-98a0-473c-8c60-8c02b3bbd06c" />

- Screenshot: [Dune]
  > Top: Cumulated Supply USD per market
  > Bottom: Cumulated Borrow USD per market
- Caption: By overlaying borrow and supply trends across specific periods (e.g., Oct-Dec 2024), we calculate utilization = borrow/supply to identify capital efficiency patterns
  → 6th January, 2026:
  > Utilization Rate = $1.6B ÷ $1.9B × 100
                 = 0.842 × 100
                 = 84.2%
  -------ADD IMAGE
- Insight: 84.2% utilization = Strong Capital Efficiency
>Good for lenders: Their deposits are earning yield (not sitting idle)
>Risk signal for borrowers: Limited capacity for new large borrows without adding more supply
>BD implication: Protocol is near capacity → prioritize supply-side partnerships (attract more depositors) to enable further borrow growth

##### Context Benchmarks:
> <50%: Underutilized (too much idle capital)

> 50-70%: Healthy range

> 70-85%: High efficiency (optimal)

>85%: Risk zone (liquidity crunch possible)
